Evidence-based XAI Foundations

This guide maps EduXAI Studio criteria to technical and pedagogical foundations from current literature and policy-oriented resources.

New mandatory blocks in schema v2

New Version xai-exercises/2.0.0 includes two mandatory blocks: xai.human_oversight and xai.quality_of_explanation.
