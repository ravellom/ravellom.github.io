Exe Builder XAI Help

Practical guide to create, explain (XAI), edit, and export exercises for teachers and for the interactive visor.

Recommended flow

  1. Enter your API Key and choose a model.
  2. Set the exercise count (recommended for testing: 3).
  3. Paste content or upload a summary (.txt, .md, .json, .docx, .pdf).
  4. Generate with AI and review the Exercise Explorer (list + detail).
  5. Edit content, interaction, and XAI fields.
  6. Validate and export depending on your target (teacher project or visor package).

What XAI output is returned?

Each exercise includes an xai block with pedagogical rationale and traceability.

why_this_exercisepedagogical_alignmentcontent_selectiondesign_rationalefairness_and_riskhuman_oversightquality_of_explanationuncertaintycounterfactualtrace
  • pedagogical_alignment: objective, competency, Bloom, and difficulty.
  • content_selection: why this content and source grounding.
  • fairness_and_risk + uncertainty: potential biases, mitigations, confidence limits.
  • human_oversight: teacher review and final human decision authority.
  • quality_of_explanation: audience, clarity, and actionability of explanations.
  • counterfactual: what should change if context/conditions change.
  • trace: model, prompt, timestamp audit trail.

This supports auditability of instructional decisions, not only question generation.
